Building Future Leaders

Mission Ekalavya goes beyond academics and focuses on holistic development of students. We believe that future civil servants need more than just bookish knowledge - they need strong character, leadership abilities, and excellent communication skills.

🎯 Key Areas of Personality Development:

  • Public Speaking: Regular speaking exercises, debates, and presentations to overcome stage fear
  • Communication Skills: Written and verbal communication training for effective expression
  • Leadership Skills: Team leading exercises, group projects, and decision-making scenarios
  • Time Management: Effective study planning, prioritization, and execution strategies
  • Team Building: Collaborative learning, group discussions, and peer mentoring
  • Confidence Building: Motivational sessions, positive reinforcement, and success stories
  • Interview Skills: Mock interviews, body language training, and feedback sessions
  • Emotional Intelligence: Self-awareness, empathy training, and stress management

📅 Activities Include:

  • Weekly group discussions on current topics
  • Monthly debate competitions with certificates
  • Leadership workshops by industry experts
  • Personality assessment and personalized feedback
  • Goal setting and progress tracking sessions
  • Extempore speaking practice
  • Role-playing exercises for real-life scenarios
