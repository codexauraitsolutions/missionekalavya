Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Mission Ekalavya

What is Mission Ekalavya?
Mission Ekalavya is India's first structured UPSC foundation program designed specifically for school students from Class 6 to Class 12, integrating NCERT learning with competitive exam preparation.
Who can join this program?
Students currently studying in Classes 6 to 12 can join Mission Ekalavya. We have different batches for different grade levels.
Is the program completely online?
Yes, Mission Ekalavya is a fully online program with live interactive classes, recorded sessions, and online tests.
Are recordings available if I miss a class?
Yes, all live classes are recorded and available in your student dashboard for later viewing.
How are tests conducted?
Weekly and monthly tests are conducted online through our platform. Students receive instant results and detailed performance analysis.
What is the course duration?
We offer two programs: 18-month program for Classes 6-10 and 36-month comprehensive program for Classes 6-12.
Is mentorship provided?
Yes, students receive regular mentorship sessions from experienced faculty and guest lectures from IAS officers.
How can parents track progress?
Parents receive monthly progress reports, can access the parent dashboard, and schedule parent-teacher meetings.
What is the fee structure?
18-month program: ₹45,000 | 36-month program: ₹75,000. EMI options and early bird discounts available.
Is there a scholarship test?
Yes, we conduct a scholarship test for meritorious students. Top performers can get up to 100% fee waiver.
What is the batch size?
We maintain small batch sizes of 30-40 students to ensure personalized attention and effective doubt clearing.
Do you provide study materials?
Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials, notes, and practice questions for all subjects.
Can I switch between programs?
Yes, you can upgrade from 18-month to 36-month program within the first 3 months by paying the difference amount.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee after the first class if you are not satisfied with the program.
What technology do I need?
You need a smartphone, tablet, or laptop with a stable internet connection. Our platform works on all devices.
